About Us

Jain Group of Institutions ( JGI ) has significant experience in running “best-in-class” educational institutions over the past two decades. JGI is perhaps the only educational group in India which has the experience of running excellent, world class institutions in all categories viz. K-12 (Kindergarten to 12th standard), Pre-University, Undergraduate(including Science, Engineering, and Technology) and Post graduate. The offerings are also highly integrated, ensuring that student learners are able to build successive layers of skills, knowledge and attitude.
